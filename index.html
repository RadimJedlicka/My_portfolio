About me

With the rapid development of technology, I have watched with fascination how the world around is changing and the possibilities of programming are much easier today. Edinburgh Bikes data analysis

In Edinburgh, as in other cities, the "bike sharing" system works - there are stations with bikes in the city, you can borrow one and then return it at another station. My task was to process the relevant data and find out useful information from it. I focused on active and inactive stations identification, most frequented stations identification, calculating the distances between each station, how long does one ride lasted. Large part of the analyses was visualization of demand development over time and weather effect on bike rentals.
